About Us

Learn more about EczeCare!

  • Analyze skin disease

    The user can check the type of skin disease by uploading the image. Also, they can check out the necessary steps and precautions to take by clicking on the Analyze tab!

  • Severity Check

    The uploaded image is passed through segmentation model for detecting the spread of eczema by considering area factor.Our Model uses Computer Vision to Recognize type of eczema from the initial appearance of symptoms on skin.

  • Consult with doctors

    Option to consult with doctors where users can communicate with doctors virtually or schedule an appointment.

  • Healing progress & Skin Maintenance

    Users can track progress of their skin condition with regular uploading of skin images and can get interactive analysis. Users can have reminders and suggestions related to their eczema type and severity.

  • Explore Blogs!

    This platform can unlock new ways for people to stay better informed about skin disorders through expert-curated blogs.
